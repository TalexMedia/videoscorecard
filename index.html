Dimension 01 Audience Pull

Video made for everyone gets watched by no one. How well do you actually know the person on the other side of the screen?

2
Dimension 02 Watchability

People choose what they watch. A video has a few seconds to prove it is worth the time, or it gets scrolled past.

3
Dimension 03 Story

Facts get forgotten. Stories get retold. The brands people remember are the ones that gave them a story worth passing on.

4
Dimension 04 Episodic

A single video is a moment. A series is a habit. Audiences are built by showing up with something they can follow.

5
Dimension 05 Presence

Trust is built by showing up again and again, not by one good video. The audience needs to know you'll be back before they invest their attention.
